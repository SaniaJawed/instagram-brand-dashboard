Myths vs reality

Click each myth to reveal what the data actually shows.

Myth

You need 100K followers to make money from Instagram

Reality

10K followers with 6%+ engagement earns more than 200K with 0.5%. Brands pay for real attention, not vanity numbers.

Myth

Posting every day is what grows your account

Reality

3 high-quality posts/week outperform 7 mediocre ones. Algorithm rewards saves and shares — not frequency.

Myth

Brand deals are the main income stream for creators

Reality

Top creators earn 60–70% from their own products — not brand deals. Your own product is an asset you own forever.

Myth

Going viral = growing your brand

Reality

Viral posts bring off-niche followers who don't convert. The best brands grow slowly with the right 1,000 people.
